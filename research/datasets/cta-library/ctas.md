### Key Observations
1. **First-person CTAs** ("My", "My Project") are increasing in Arabic — a shift from traditional formal language
2. **Bilingual CTAs** often place Arabic first, especially on Saudi sites
3. **Short CTAs** (2-3 words in both languages) outperform longer ones
4. **Trust-based CTAs** are far more prevalent than Western markets — Saudi users need trust signals before conversion
5. **"احصل على" (Get)** is the most versatile CTA opener in Arabic
6. **Religious/cultural cues** are subtle — no direct religious references but "بإذن الله" (God willing) appears in some softer CTAs
