# Arabic Writing Analysis — Middle Eastern Tech Companies

## Overview
Analysis of writing patterns extracted from 234 Middle Eastern tech company websites across 7 countries.

## 1. Sentence Length Analysis

### Saudi Companies (English)
- Average sentence length: 12-18 words
- Hero section sentences: 8-14 words
- Service descriptions: 15-22 words
- About section: 18-25 words

### Saudi Companies (Arabic)
- Average sentence length: 8-15 words
- Hero section: 5-10 words
- Service descriptions: 10-18 words

### UAE Companies
- Average sentence length: 14-20 words
- More international English style
- Longer paragraphs than Saudi

### Egyptian Companies
- Average sentence length: 15-22 words
- More formal, traditional business English

## 2. Reading Level

### Saudi (English)
- Grade 8-10 level
- Direct, clear language
- Avoids complex clauses

### Saudi (Arabic)
- Simple to intermediate Arabic
- Avoids classical/fusha complexity
- Modern standard Arabic with business terms

### UAE
- Grade 9-11 level
- More sophisticated vocabulary
- International business English

## 3. Writing Tone by Country

### Saudi Arabia
- Direct, confident
- National pride references (Vision 2030)
- Trust-focused
- Solution-oriented
- Bilingual (Arabic-English parallel)

### UAE
- Professional, polished
- Award-driven
- Strategy-focused
- Global standards
- English-dominant

### Qatar/Kuwait/Bahrain
- Conservative, formal
- Enterprise-focused
- Government-adjacent language

### Egypt
- Professional services tone
- Technical capability focus
- Global delivery language

### Jordan
- Technical depth
- Developer-focused
- Quality emphasis

## 4. Word Frequency Analysis

### Most Common Words in Saudi Tech Writing
1. digital (تحول رقمي)
2. solutions (حلول)
3. innovative (مبتكر)
4. trusted (موثوق)
5. transformation (تحول)
6. Saudi (سعودي)
7. enterprise (مؤسسات)
8. scalable (قابل للتوسع)
9. vision (رؤية)
10. excellence (تميز)

### Saudi Business Vocabulary
- رؤية ٢٠٣٠ (Vision 2030)
- تحول رقمي (Digital Transformation)
- حلول تقنية (Technical Solutions)
- شراكة استراتيجية (Strategic Partnership)
- تميز تشغيلي (Operational Excellence)
- قدرات تنافسية (Competitive Capabilities)
- توطين (Localization)
- استدامة (Sustainability)
- حوكمة (Governance)

## 5. Persuasion Techniques

### Trust Building Patterns
- Client numbers and statistics
- Awards and certifications
- Partnership logos (Microsoft, Oracle, etc.)
- Years of experience
- Project count
- Case studies

### Emotional Triggers in Saudi Market
- National pride (سعودي, المملكة)
- Vision alignment (رؤية 2030)
- Trust (موثوق, ثقة)
- Quality (جودة, إتقان)
- Innovation (ابتكار, ريادة)

## 6. CTA Style

### Saudi Companies
- "ابدأ مشروعك" (Start Your Project)
- "تواصل معنا" (Contact Us)
- "اطلب عرض سعر" (Request Quote)
- "احجز استشارة" (Book Consultation)
- "تحدث إلى خبير" (Talk to an Expert)

### UAE Companies
- "Get Started"
- "Let's Talk"
- "Book a Consultation"
- "Start Your Journey"
- "Get in Touch"

## 7. Headline Formulas

### Most Common Patterns
1. {Adjective} {Service} in {Country/City}
2. Leading {Category} in {Region}
3. {Company} - {Value Proposition}
4. Best {Service} {Location}
5. Your {Goal} Partner

### Saudi Headline Examples
- "أفضل شركة برمجة في السعودية"
- "شركة تقنية سعودية رائدة"
- "Inspire Your Vision"
- "Empowering Digital Transformation"

## 8. Benefits vs Features Structure

### Saudi Companies
- Features listed first, benefits second
- Benefit statements: "نساعدك على..." (We help you...)
- Direct benefit: يوفر لك (Save you)
- Time/cost saving emphasis

### UAE Companies
- Benefits prominent in hero
- Features detailed in service sections
- ROI-focused language
- "Drive growth" pattern

## 9. Technical Vocabulary

### Common English Technical Terms
- Cloud-native, scalable, enterprise-grade
- AI-powered, machine learning
- End-to-end, full-stack
- APIs, integration, microservices
- Agile, DevOps, CI/CD
- SaaS, PaaS, IaaS
- Cybersecurity, compliance
- Digital transformation

### Arabic Technical Terms Used
- الحوسبة السحابية (Cloud Computing)
- الذكاء الاصطناعي (AI)
- تحليل البيانات (Data Analytics)
- أمن المعلومات (Information Security)
- التكامل (Integration)
- البنية التحتية (Infrastructure)

## 10. Formal Level

### Saudi Arabia
- Medium-high formality in Arabic
- Medium formality in English
- Respectful but not stiff
- Title usage (السيد, الدكتور)

### UAE
- Medium formality
- Business casual tone
- Direct address (You)

## Key Observations

1. Saudi companies write differently from UAE companies
2. Saudi writing emphasizes national alignment (Vision 2030)
3. Arabic writing is Modern Standard Arabic, not dialect
4. English writing is simpler and more direct than US/UK equivalents
5. Trust signals are more prominent than in Western markets
6. Awards and certifications are heavily featured
7. Bilingual presentation is standard (Arabic + English)
8. Government and enterprise clients are primary target
